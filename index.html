Privacy Policy

At ClipStash Lite, we believe your data belongs to you and only you. Our commitment to privacy is unwavering and built into every aspect of our product.

Our Commitment

ClipStash Lite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All clipboard content is stored locally in your browser and is never sent to any server.

No tracking, no ads, no analytics. 100% local and private.

Data Security

Your clipboard data remains on your device at all times. We have designed ClipStash Lite with privacy as our top priority, ensuring that your sensitive information stays where it belongs - with you.

  • No server storage or transmission
  • Browser-based local storage only
  • No third-party access to your data
